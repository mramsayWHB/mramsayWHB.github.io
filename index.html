What Is This Fundraiser?

This fundraiser is to raise money for Canine Companions. Canine Companions is a non-profit organization which provides service and facility dogs to schools and people with disabilities at no charge. Canine Companions relies only on donations.

Why Should You Donate?

Canine Companions donated Oric to the school for no cost at all. We would like to give back to Canine Companions to support them and thank them for their generous donation. You can also win prizes depending on how much your sponspors donate.

How to Raise Funds

Now that the warm weather is here, we want you to get outside and walk like Oric - in and out of school! Starting June 1st we will have opportunities in school for students to walk with Oric. To donate, find sponsors to help reach your donation goal (amounts listed on the donation site). Let's get moving!
